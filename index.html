IDMODEINOUT
Single Mode (SMF)
Core: ~9µm. Supports only the fundamental axial mode. Light travels straight down the center. Zero intermodal dispersion. Used for long-distance/undersea cables.
Step Index (MMF)
Core: ~50-100µm. Uniform refractive index. Light reflects sharply via Total Internal Reflection (TIR). High dispersion creates signal pulse spreading.
Graded Index (GRIN)
Core: ~50-62.5µm. Refractive index decreases parabolically from center. Light travels in sinusoidal waves. Reduces dispersion compared to Step Index.
